Three games that inspired me to become a developer

Grand Theft auto III

When Claude Speed, a young robber and street racer gets betrayed by his Girlfriend Catalina, leader of the columbian cartel during a Heist. He is arrested but escapes shortly after, He decides to exact his revenge on Catalina, in order to do so he must work his way up in the Liberty City Underworld and stop Catalina.

Grand Theft auto
Vice City

Set in 1986 within the fictional Vice City (based on Miami and Miami Beach), the single-player story follows mobster Tommy Vercetti's rise to power after being released from prison and becoming caught up in an ambushed drug deal.

Grand Theft auto
San Andreas

After five years on the east coast, Carl Johnson returns to the city of Los Santos, San Andreas to find numerous fellow gang-members dead and enemy gangs having dominance of the city. Carl must take over the city and get his old gang back on top before the city destroys itself.
